About me

My name is Valeriia Zarubina. I'm almost 26 y.o. and I was born and raised in Voronezh, which is the city in Russia. As we used to say: "It is a small town not so far from Moscow". Actually, we have much more than 1 000 000 citizens and it takes 6 hours by car from Moscow to us. As I can remember myself,I was always obsessed with travels and new cities, new countries. As I've visited quite a lot of, I've also made a list of Tips, that were very useful for me and maybe will be also useful for you, guys. Please enjoy!


Tips

  1. Be Flexible. We always plan for delays and try not to get upset when things inevitably go wrong.
  2. Make a Plan.
  3. Learn Common Phrases of the Local Language.
  4. Don't Forget an Extra Camera Battery (or Two)
  5. Always Buy Travel Insurance.
  6. Make Photocopies of Important Documents.
  7. Pack Extra Underwear.
  8. Have extra money on your credit card.
